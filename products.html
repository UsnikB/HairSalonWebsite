COLORPHLEX

Colorphlex is manufactured by Earthly Body, a global leader in natural personal care products since 1996. HUMBLE BEGINNINGS In 1996, Kevin and Mare Wachs started creating quality natural personal care products in their garage. Since then, Earthly body, the manufacturer of colorpHlex, which started as a respected family owned company, has grown into a global leader in natural personal care products. In 2013 Earthly Body moved its corporate headquarters into a 50,000 sq ft building in Chatsworth CA that today serves as their manufacturing and bottling facility, shipping warehouse, corporate offices and International Education Center for all of its brands. This building is a testament to the commitment that the Wachs family & Earthly Body team has made to our planet’s sustainability. All aspects of their organization reflect eco-friendly values, from the use of recyclable packaging and planting trees for the future, to the installation of both solar panels and electric car charging stations, the company iscommitted to reducing their carbon footprint.
