WHAT WE DO

The Beauty Parlour offers a full range of salon treatments and styling services provided by a team of professional stylists, all in a clean air environment. As a clean air salon, we offer ammonia-free colour services with little to no scent properties or chemical emanation. This healthier alternative to traditional colouring practices is also safe for expecting mothers and individuals with allergies or scent sensitivities.

WEDDING INQUIRIES

If you are looking to celebrate your special day with us, please note that on Saturdays we only have two to three stylists on, which can make it difficult to cater to big parties. We are able to start earlier than 10am if needed, and if your stylist does not normally work Saturdays, feel free to ask them if they will come in for you on your big day. Any other questions about Bridal bookings contact Raquel at organicbeautyparlour@gmail.com.
